CustomerReview

Sophia Bauer

FoodF&B's Burger:

FoodF&B is one of the most famous Burger restaurants in the city, loved by many diners for its delicious taste and professional service style. Among the Burger dishes here, the "Fire-Grilled American Beef" Burger is the perfect choice for those who want a quick, delicious and nutritious lunch.

Noah Williams

FoodF&B's Pepperoni Pizza:

The "4 Kinds of Cheese" Pizza is quite large in size, up to 30cm in diameter, suitable for 4-5 people to enjoy. The Pizza is baked crispy, golden brown, and has a delicious aroma. The top of the cake is covered with a generous layer of cheese including mozzarella, cheddar, parmesan and blue cheese, creating an irresistible greasy and delicious flavor.

Aarya Patel

FoodF&B's Juice:

"Vitamin C" juice at FoodF&B provides a cool, sour taste that is easy to drink. However, the minus point of this drink is its strong sweetness, which can cause discomfort for those who do not like sweet foods. Besides, the price of "Vitamin C" juice is considered high compared to its quality.

Marco Bianchi

FoodF&B's Korean Food:

"Kimchi jjigae" at FoodF&B has the characteristic spicy taste of Kimchi, combined with rich broth and fresh ingredients such as pork belly, tofu, mushrooms, and vegetables. The food is beautifully presented, providing an impressive Korean culinary experience. However, the spiciness of Kimchi jjigae may be too strong for those who don't like spicy food.
